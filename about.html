About

MISSION

To support young athletes living in Chicago northside neigthborhoods, who have a interest in learning and playing soccer with opportunities to learn and practice skills related to the game of soccer specifically those skills around team cooperation and good sportsmanship.

VISION

The Northside Your Soccer League aspires to developed strong ,well- rounded and mindful athletes through the building of character self- discipline and leadership

GENERAL INFORMATION

The Northside Your Soccer League was established in 1996 to provide athletes residing in Chicago northside neighborhoods an environment in which to learn and play soccer.To be a member of NYSL , you must be between the ages of 4-12 and reside in a Chicago northside neighborhood. NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
